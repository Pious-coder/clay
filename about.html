Adeoti Azeez Afeez (d.clay.guy)

Adeoti Azeez Afeez is a sgraffito ceramicist whose drawing on englobed-coated clay reveals a contrasting colour. This drawing,usually done with cross-hatching,adds depth,tone and chiaroscuro to his works. He is a graduate of Yaba College of Tecchnology and has participated in a number of group exhibitions including Beyond Limit 2022,LIMCAF 2022,Colour Theory,AMG Projects 2023 and (Sur)face exhibition at Chilli Art Project 2023. Adeoti also participated in the Statues Also Breath workshop with Prune Nourry,in collaboration with Obafemi Awolowo University,Ile Ife.
